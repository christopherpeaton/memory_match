Description

This game is a simple match game, but I chose to use cars as the topic. I love cars. All types, sizes, vintage, air cooled, water cooled, american, german, italian, british, japanese. To me it doesn't matter, as long as it goes, and it goes well. For as long as I can remember I have had an interest in cars, and this symbolizes that passion.

Skills needed for project completion

For the completion of this project, a knowledge of several areas was necessary. Base knowledge of html/css was a must, but also the knowledge to look for/find how to do things I didn't know how to do. Google is my friend. How to work a text editor was a must, as was time management. Basic understanding of bootstrap, and some javascript was also helpful.
